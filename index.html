About Me

With over 12 years of experience as a Service Provider Network Engineer, my expertise encompasses IP transmission (via microwave PTP/PTMP and fiber [DWDM, xPON]), backhaul network planning, and the architecture of large-scale service provider networks. My technical background covers routing (OSPF, IS-IS, BGP, EIGRP, Segment Routing), switching, MPLS, VPN, QoS, and network security, with proficiency in equipment from industry leaders such as Ericsson, Cisco, and Huawei. I am highly skilled in network automation and programmability (Ansible, Python [Netmiko/Paramiko]) and hold certifications in AI, machine learning, and data analysis.

Alongside my professional responsibilities, I am deeply committed to research that bridges cutting-edge industry practice and academic innovation. My current interests lie in deterministic networking (DetNet/TSN) for O‑RAN xHaul, bounded‑latency transport over SRv6, and performance‑aware service orchestration at the edge. I explore how heterogeneous transport domains – from fronthaul to edge cloud – can provide predictable delay and jitter guarantees for next‑generation O‑RAN and 6G systems. My earlier contributions include multiple recognised publications in IEEE journals, magazines, and conferences on O‑RAN security, AI/ML, and blockchain.
