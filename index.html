About Me

I am a telecom/network engineer with 10+ years of experience in service-provider and enterprise networks, focusing on design, optimization, and deployment. My current research centers on Zero-Trust security for O-RAN, blockchain-based supply-chain verification, and applied machine learning for cybersecurity.
