About Me

I am a service provider network engineer with over 12 years of hands‑on experience in IP transmission (microwave PTP/PTMP and fiber [DWDM, xPON]), backhaul planning, and large‑scale network architecture. My technical toolkit spans routing (OSPF, IS-IS, BGP, EIGRP, Segment Routing), switching, MPLS, VPNs, QoS, and security, and I work comfortably with gear from Ericsson, Cisco, and Huawei. I’m also deep into network automation with Ansible and Python (Netmiko/Paramiko) and keep my skills sharp with certifications in AI, machine learning, and data analysis.

My research passion lies squarely in deterministic networking and Time‑Sensitive Networking (DetNet/TSN) – that’s the core of what I tinker with and publish on. I explore how to bring bounded‑latency transport over SRv6 and performance‑aware service orchestration into O‑RAN xHaul and edge‑cloud environments, aiming for predictable delay and jitter guarantees in 6G systems. Alongside that main thread, I’ve contributed to multiple IEEE venues on O‑RAN security, AI/ML, and blockchain.
